Perfect Lasagna

Description:

This amazing lasagna is all cheesy goodness and delicious meatiness. A single bite and you're transported to a simpler times. Times before it all went wrong. It's also garfield's favorite :D, so make sure you come on in and learn to make the family favorite!

Ingredients:

  • Beef
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
