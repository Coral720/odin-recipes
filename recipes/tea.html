Addison Tea

Description:

Learn to make this amazing tea from the comfort of your own home! Save yourself a couple of quarters and get to brewing this wonderfully soothing tea.

Ingredients:

  • Poppy Seeds
  • Lemons
  • Water
  • Infectious Darkness

Steps:

  1. Set a pot on the stove and pour your water in
  2. Wait until the water reaches a boil before turning off the heat
  3. Soak seeds in the boiled water for 10 minutes
  4. Strain out the seeds and pour tea into mug of choice
  5. Squeeze fresh lemon juice into tea
  6. Don't forget the darkness! All will sucumb.
